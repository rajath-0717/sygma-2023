Rules & Regulations


  1. A team consist of 7 participants.No participant can participate in more than one event
  2. A team has to participate in all the events to be in the contention for the Overall Championship.
  3. Registration has to be done through our website https://sygma.sdm.ac.in. Registration fee of Rs. 350/- per team shall be payable ON SPOT.
  4. The fest is open to all the Computer Science and Computer Application students. .
  5. Teams have to confirm their participation via email or through call to the incharge student coordinator. Email-sygma@sdmcbm.ac.in
  6. Participants are requested to produce their identity cards and Bonafide Certificate at the Time of Registration.
  7. Punctuality and discipline are of primary concern. Offensive and abusive actions will not be entertained.
  8. The decisions of the judges & staff coordinator will be final and binding by all regards.
  9. The team should report at the registration desk before 8:30 A.M.
  10. Organizers have the right to reschedule or modify an event, if necessary.
  11. Cash prize will be awarded to the winners of events.
